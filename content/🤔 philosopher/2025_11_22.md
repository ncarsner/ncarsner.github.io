# Emptiness

We all encounter it, whether it be with a blank sheet of paper, the seed of an idea in our mind, a clean whiteboard, <i>etc.</i> The frame is the the boundary. The unadorned center mandates the expectation of creative contribution. With enough practice, we learn to suppress, diminish, or ignore the feeling of intimidation that comes with the domination of the blank space over what we have contributed. Until then, it is intimdating.

One of my favorite things about writing code is that there is a solution that exists to the creative effort. It arrives if I sequence the right volume and combination of characters into the environment shell and call the computer to interpret the code.

```bash
mkdir new-project
cd new-project
```

The new project directory is created.<br>
I have moved into the project directory.
